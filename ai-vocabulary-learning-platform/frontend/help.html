Frequently Asked Questions

Go to the Dashboard and click "Start Your Lessons". You will be taken to the Lessons page where you can choose a lesson and begin answering questions.

Your progress is based on completed lessons.

  • A lesson is marked Completed only if you answer all questions correctly.
  • If you miss questions, you may need to restart and try again.
  • Completed lessons will show a completion mark on the Lessons page.

Level progress updates every time you reach certain milestones.

To complete a lesson:

  • Select a lesson from the Lessons page
  • Answer all questions in the lesson
  • If all answers are correct, the lesson will be marked Completed

If the lesson is not completed, you can restart it and try again.

Each lesson contains a set of vocabulary questions designed to test your understanding of words and meanings.

Answer all questions correctly to successfully complete the lesson.

Yes. If you do not complete a lesson successfully, then you can restart the lesson and try again.

This helps you practice and improve your vocabulary learning.

If a lesson is not fully completed:

  • The lesson will not be marked as completed
  • You can restart the lesson and try again
  • Once all questions are answered correctly, the lesson will show as completed

After finishing a lesson, your score will be displayed automatically.

  • Your score shows how many questions you answered correctly.
  • This helps you understand your performance in the lesson.
  • You can restart the lesson anytime to try and improve your score.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Sign in or create your account
  2. After signing in, choose your learning language and level
  3. Go to Dashboard and start your first lesson
  4. Answer all questions in a lesson
  5. Complete lessons to track your learning progress
